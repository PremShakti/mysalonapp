Welcome to My salon App or Aries, your premier destination for beauty and relaxation. Our cutting-edge salon website, powered by the robust MERN stack, is designed to offer you a seamless and immersive experience from the moment you land on our page.

**Explore Our Services:**
Discover a world of beauty and wellness with our comprehensive range of services. From trendy haircuts and stunning color treatments to rejuvenating facials and soothing massages, our skilled professionals are committed to enhancing your natural beauty and leaving you feeling refreshed and revitalized.

**Intuitive Booking System:**
Booking your next salon experience is a breeze with our user-friendly interface. Our MERN-powered website features an intuitive booking system that allows you to schedule appointments at your convenience. Choose your preferred stylist, select your desired service, and pick a time that suits your busy lifestyle.

**Virtual Consultations:**
Can't make it to the salon in person? No problem! Our website offers virtual consultations, allowing you to connect with our experienced stylists and therapists from the comfort of your home. Discuss your preferences, get personalized recommendations, and plan your salon experience with ease.

**Real-time Updates:**
Stay informed about your appointments and receive real-time updates through our website. Whether it's a reminder about your upcoming session or information about the latest promotions, we keep you in the loop so you never miss out on the pampering you deserve.

**Gallery and Testimonials:**
Immerse yourself in the world of beauty by exploring our gallery showcasing the incredible transformations we've achieved for our clients. Read testimonials from satisfied customers who have experienced the magic of our skilled team.

**Secure and Convenient Payment:**
Our MERN stack ensures the security of your transactions, making the payment process seamless and stress-free. Enjoy the convenience of secure online payments as you finalize your salon experience.

**Blog and Trends:**
Stay up-to-date with the latest beauty trends, tips, and industry insights through our blog. We love sharing our expertise with you, keeping you informed about the hottest styles and beauty innovations.

My salon App or Aries is not just a salon; it's a destination for self-care and rejuvenation. Experience the perfect blend of technology and beauty at your fingertips. Book your appointment now and let us transform your vision of beauty into reality.

### `npm run eject`

**Note: this is a one-way operation. Once you `eject`, you can't go back!**

If you aren't satisfied with the build tool and configuration choices, you can `eject` at any time. This command will remove the single build dependency from your project.

Instead, it will copy all the configuration files and the transitive dependencies (webpack, Babel, ESLint, etc) right into your project so you have full control over them. All of the commands except `eject` will still work, but they will point to the copied scripts so you can tweak them. At this point you're on your own.

You don't have to ever use `eject`. The curated feature set is suitable for small and middle deployments, and you shouldn't feel obligated to use this feature. However we understand that this tool wouldn't be useful if you couldn't customize it when you are ready for it.
